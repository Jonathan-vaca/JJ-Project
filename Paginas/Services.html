Our Services

At J.Jr Signs, we deliver full-service solutions for the sign and graphics industry. From design and fabrication to installation and maintenance, we ensure every project is executed with precision, durability, and visual impact.

Custom Sign Fabrication

We specialize in designing and manufacturing tailor-made signage that aligns with your brand identity and architectural requirements. Using CNC routing, laser cutting, and precision finishing, we produce signs that are both visually striking and built to last.

Vehicle Wraps & Fleet Graphics

Our vehicle graphics division provides complete solutions for cars, trucks, vans, and fleet vehicles. From full and partial wraps to spot graphics, we use premium vinyl materials and advanced lamination techniques to ensure long-lasting, fade-resistant results.

Architectural & Environmental Graphics

Enhance your interior and exterior spaces with wall murals, window films, dimensional lettering, and wayfinding systems. We create branded environments that improve customer experience and reinforce your corporate identity.

Illuminated & LED Signage

We design and install energy-efficient illuminated signs, including LED channel letters, cabinet signs, and backlit displays. All lighting systems are engineered for maximum visibility, low maintenance, and compliance with local electrical codes.

Channel Letters & Dimensional Signage

Our custom channel letters are fabricated with precision using aluminum, acrylic, and LED illumination options. We also produce non-illuminated dimensional letters and logos, available in a variety of finishes such as brushed metal, PVC, and acrylic.

Large Format Printing & Banners

From promotional banners to trade show displays, our high-resolution printing capabilities allow us to produce vibrant, durable graphics in virtually any size. We use UV-resistant inks and heavy-duty substrates suitable for both indoor and outdoor applications.

Monument & Pylon Signs

We design and build free-standing monument and pylon signs that deliver maximum visibility for businesses and institutions. All structures are engineered for durability, weather resistance, and compliance with local zoning requirements.

Installation & Maintenance

Our certified installation team ensures your signage is safely and securely installed, whether on building facades, rooftops, or freestanding structures. We also provide scheduled maintenance, LED retrofits, and repair services to keep your signs in optimal condition.

Design & Project Management

From concept development to permitting and final installation, we manage every stage of your signage project. Our in-house design team works with CAD drawings, 3D renderings, and proofing systems to guarantee accuracy and quality.
